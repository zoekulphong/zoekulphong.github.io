tryviridia.com

redesigning a complete plant care company's website

Tryviridia.com highlights features of the Viridia mobile app and provides background and contact information for Viridia.

challenge

As a small company, Viridia had not yet invested in true design work. Their previous website design was outdated and left viewers confused about the scope of the company. This design also had no explanations for Viridia’s primary product, the Viridia mobile app, and had no clear calls to action.

The main purpose of tryviridia.com is to inform viewers of the many features of the Viridia mobile app. In order to do so, the website must highlight and explain the benefits of the app, while also providing a clear call to action to download the app to see more.

Additionally, tryviridia.com provides background information and about Viridia’s roots to give more insight into Viridia’s goals and contact information for other plant enthusiasts to reach out.
